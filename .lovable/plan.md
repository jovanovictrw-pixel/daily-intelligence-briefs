

# Vesper — AI Market Intelligence Landing Page

## Overview
A premium editorial dark landing page for Vesper, an AI market intelligence platform. Design inspired by The Economist meets Bloomberg Terminal — serious, trustworthy, intelligent.

## Design System Setup
- **Colors**: Near-black background (#0A0C10), surface (#111318), gold accent (#C9A84C), steel blue (#4A7FA5), warm off-white text (#F0EDE6)
- **Typography**: Playfair Display (headlines), IBM Plex Sans (body), IBM Plex Mono (data labels) via Google Fonts
- **Spacing & Layout**: Asymmetric editorial grids, generous horizontal rules, small-caps section labels

## Sections (10 total)

### 1. Sticky Navbar
Logo "VESPER" with crescent moon SVG, nav links, gold-bordered CTA. Backdrop blur + border on scroll.

### 2. Hero (60/40 split)
Large editorial headline, subheadline, dual CTAs. Right side: animated briefing card mockup with typewriter effect cycling through intel bullet points. Faint radial gold gradient + dot-grid texture background.

### 3. Logos Bar
Six fake company wordmarks (Arcola, Meridian Labs, Stratum, Delphi, Crestline, Vantis) in muted opacity with horizontal rules.

### 4. Problem Section ("01 — THE PROBLEM")
Three-column stat layout with count-up animation on scroll. Large serif numbers, divider lines, explanatory text.

### 5. How It Works ("02 — HOW IT WORKS")
Three-step horizontal flow: Connect → Compile → Deliver. Connected with dashed lines, gold step numbers, line-art SVG icons.

### 6. Features ("03 — CAPABILITIES")
Asymmetric bento grid with 6 feature cards. Gold accent borders on hover with lift effect. Role-Personalized Briefs, Competitor Tracking, Buyer Signals, Ask Vesper, CRM Enrichment, Executive Digest.

### 7. Integrations ("04 — INTEGRATIONS")
Grid of 8 integration badges (Slack, Salesforce, HubSpot, Gmail, Notion, Linear, Gong, ZoomInfo) with icon + name. "Plus 200+ sources" subline.

### 8. Social Proof
Large serif pull quote with attribution. Side metrics grid (3.2x faster response, 89% daily read rate, 41% win rate increase).

### 9. Pricing ("05 — PRICING")
Three cards: Starter ($299/mo), Growth ($799/mo, gold-bordered recommended), Enterprise (custom). Feature lists with gold checkmarks.

### 10. CTA + Footer
Radial gold glow background, large closing headline, email input + CTA button. Footer with logo, nav, social links, legal.

## Animations
- Fade-up on scroll for all sections (Intersection Observer)
- Typewriter loop on hero briefing card
- Count-up animation on problem stats
- Hover lift + gold border on all cards
- Nav CTA shimmer on load
- Fully responsive, mobile-first
